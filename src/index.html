Implementation Notes

WARNING!
This endpoint permanently deletes the budget from the storage facility. The deletion is permanent, and unrecoverable. There is no undo. Generally, your implementation should close a budget, rather than deleting it.

Members are not the same as users. Users must be first created on the public website. After they are confirmed they are created as members. This prevents public having unlimited access to create memebers, etc.

Also note... user email is not visible via the member API. This is hides the member's email from potentially public budgets. We dont' want to expose that. Thus, the user's email is handeled through the private user managment facilities.

Thus, the member routes are based on a static members collection. The fields such as PUT, POST, etc operate on the attributes of the supplied idMember, username, or the id of the authentication token.

Implementation Notes

Like a savage tigress that tossing in the jungle overlays her own cubs, so the sea dashes even the mightiest whales against the rocks, and leaves them there side by side with the split wrecks of ships. Consider the subtleness of the sea; how its most dreaded creatures glide under water, unapparent for the most part, and treacherously hidden beneath the loveliest tints of azure. Consider also the devilish brilliance and beauty of many of its most remorseless tribes, as the dainty embellished shape of many species of sharks. Consider, once more, the universal cannibalism of the sea; all whose creatures prey upon each other, carrying on eternal war since the world began.
